Share Christmas

 With Your International Friend

 

 

So many international students never enter an American home!  But, Christmas is a lovely time to share your home or traditions.  Sure, you’re busy, but invite your international friend along for these seasonal activities.  Joining you as a friend in preparing for Christmas, might be even better than being the honored guest.

 

·      Advent candle ceremony

·      Baking Christmas cookies

·      Caroling, inside or outside

·      Church and school concerts

·      Christmas shopping at decorated malls

·      Christmas tree decorating

·      Christmas tree selection or cutting

·      Setting up Christmas decorations

·      Christmas eve service

·      Christmas family meals

·      Ordinary meals and coffee dates

·      Winter walks

·      Dusting and vacuuming for your big party  J

·      Post-Christmas leftover meal

 

Gifts for your international friend could include:

 

·      Christmas story books

·      Children’s Christmas picture book

·      Christmas carol book

·      Nativity ornament

·      Your testimony or the Christmas story in simple English on decorative paper

·      A bilingual Bible or spiritual book
